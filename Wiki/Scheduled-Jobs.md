# Scheduled Jobs

Scheduled Jobs let Open Assist run recurring tasks for you.

This is useful for things like daily summaries, reminders, checks, or any task you want repeated on a schedule.

## What it can do

- Create jobs with cron schedules
- Turn jobs on or off without deleting them
- Keep job history and status
- Detect stuck runs with a built-in watchdog

## What you need

- Open Assist installed and available when the job should run
- A schedule
- A prompt that tells the assistant what to do

## How to set it up

1. Open `Settings -> Scheduled Jobs`.
2. Create a new job.
3. Give it a name.
4. Enter the schedule.
5. Add the prompt or task text.
6. Turn the job on.

## Simple examples

- "Every weekday at 9 AM, summarize my open tasks."
- "Every evening at 5 PM, draft tomorrow's plan."
- "Every Monday, check my reminders and report anything urgent."

## Good to know

- You can enable or disable a job later.
- You do not need to delete a job to pause it.
- If a job does not run, check the schedule, the enabled state, and whether the app was running.
