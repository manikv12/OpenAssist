# Privacy First Design

Open Assist is built with local-first and explicit control in mind.

## Privacy model

- No account required for normal use
- No telemetry or analytics by default
- Settings and local data stay on your Mac

## What usually stays local

- App settings
- Recent transcript history
- Learned corrections
- Conversation context
- Project and thread data
- Checkpoints and local session state

## What only leaves your Mac when you choose it

- Cloud AI providers
- Telegram remote control
- Browser and app automation requests

## Permission model

Open Assist asks for only the access it needs:

- Microphone for speech and dictation
- Accessibility for shortcuts and text insertion
- Speech Recognition only when you use Apple Speech
- Automation / Apple Events for browser and app control
- Full Disk Access only for features that need protected files or folders

## User control

You choose:

- the speech engine
- the rewrite provider
- the rewrite strength
- the automation level
- whether you want local-only AI or cloud help

Open Assist tries to do as much as possible on your Mac first.
