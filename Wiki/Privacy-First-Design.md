# Privacy First Design

Open Assist is built with local-first and explicit control principles.

## Privacy model

- No account required
- No telemetry or analytics by default
- Settings and transcript data stored locally on your Mac

## Data handling principles

- Dictation does not leave your machine unless you choose a cloud AI provider
- Clipboard copy mode is off by default to reduce clipboard history leakage
- Local AI options are available for fully on-device rewrite workflows

## What may be stored locally

- App settings
- Transcript history (recent entries)
- Learned adaptive corrections
- Conversation context used for rewrite continuity

## User control

You choose:

- Speech engine
- Rewrite provider
- Rewrite strength
- Insertion behavior

Open Assist prioritizes explicit opt-in behavior for any external model usage.
