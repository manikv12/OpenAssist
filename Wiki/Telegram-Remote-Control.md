# Telegram Remote Control

Telegram Remote Control lets you talk to Open Assist from your phone.

This is useful when you are away from your Mac but still want to check sessions, switch modes, or send a task.

## What it can do

- Pair the bot with your Open Assist setup
- Send text, voice messages, images, and audio files
- Browse projects and threads
- Switch models and backends
- Adjust reasoning effort
- Show status updates and progress messages

## Commands

- `/start`
- `/new`
- `/projects`
- `/sessions`
- `/backend`
- `/models`
- `/mode`
- `/effort`
- `/usage`
- `/status`
- `/stop`

## How to set it up

1. Create a Telegram bot with `@BotFather`.
2. Copy the bot token.
3. Open `Settings -> Telegram`.
4. Enter the token.
5. Send `/start` in Telegram to pair the bot.

## Good examples

- Ask for a short update while you are away from your desk.
- Send a voice note and let Open Assist handle it.
- Check sessions or switch models without opening your Mac.

## Good to know

- Only the paired owner should be able to control the bot.
- If it does not respond, check the token and pairing first.
- Keep the bot token private.
