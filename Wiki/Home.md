# Open Assist Wiki

![Open Assist Logo](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/manikv12/OpenAssist/main/Assets/AppLogo.png)

Open Assist is a macOS assistant with chat, voice, local or cloud AI, and approved automation.

You can use it to write, dictate, run assistant workflows, control supported apps, and keep longer work organized with projects, threads, notes, and checkpoints.

## What you will find here

- setup and first-run help
- assistant and voice workflows
- automation guidance
- AI rewrite and local AI setup
- privacy notes
- troubleshooting
